Use this site goal:
A simple youth rugby club homepage that helps parents and players understand the club and register or make contact.

Use this structure on the page:
1. Hero
   - Club name: Culpeper Youth Rugby Football Club
   - Short name: Culpeper Youth Rugby
   - CTA: Register for Summer Rugby
   - Secondary CTA: Contact the Club
   - Mention new and returning players are welcome.

2. Current Season
   - Summer 2026 Rugby
   - Practices begin May 26
   - Season kickoff June 6
   - No games July 5
   - Championships July 11
   - Note: spring 2026 registered players are already registered for summer.

3. About the Club
   - Youth rugby for Culpeper and surrounding counties
   - Pre-K through high school
   - Tag rugby for younger ages, tackle rugby for older age groups
   - Welcoming, safe, development-focused environment.

4. Principles
   Use these exact principle labels and meanings:
   - Unity: Working together towards the same goal — through triumphs and struggles.
   - Courage: Overcoming obstacles through teamwork and preparation.
   - Respect: Showing positivity to others on and off the pitch.

5. Learn About Rugby
   - Explain that rugby is beginner-friendly
   - Emphasize teamwork, movement, confidence, and respect
   - Write for skeptical parents.

6. Get Involved
   - Player
   - Parent
   - Coach
   - Volunteer
   - Sponsor
   - Supporter

7. Contact / Register
   - Create a simple non-functional placeholder form with name, email, player age/grade, and message.
   - Add a visible note: “Practice build only — replace with official registration/contact links before launch.”

Style:
- Dark navy background
- White text
- Gold accents
- Clean, confident, youth-sports feel
